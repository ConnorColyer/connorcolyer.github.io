About Me

I'm an 18-year-old innovator from London, pushing boundaries in technology and creative media. My passion lies at the intersection of cutting-edge software development and cinematic storytelling.


Currently building Spectra, an AI-powered screen-aware intelligence system that's redefining human-computer interaction. When I'm not coding, youll find me capturing the world through my lens.


Recent roles include supporting creative technology teams as a Technical Assistant at The Mill and as an Engineering Assistant with Technicolor Group in Los Angeles. I've also freelanced as a videographer for Argus Bermuda and provided lighting support for The Picture of Dorian Gray stage production.


Recognised with the Computer Science Prize at Prendergast School and an active volunteer at Greenwich Library, I bring a blend of technical expertise and community spirit to every project.


I believe in building the future we want to see – one line of code and one frame at a time.
