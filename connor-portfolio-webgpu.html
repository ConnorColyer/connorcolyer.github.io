About Me

I'm Connor Colyer, a software engineer with a passion for building innovative solutions at the intersection of technology and creativity. Based between London and Winchester, UK, I combine full-stack development expertise with professional experience in videography and theatre lighting design.


Currently preparing to begin my BSc in Computer Software Engineering at the University of Winchester (starting September 2025), I bring hands-on experience from roles at industry leaders including The Mill and Technicolor Group. My technical toolkit spans from low-level systems programming in Rust to modern web applications with React and TypeScript.


Beyond code, I'm an accomplished videographer with professional DSLR and drone experience, and have contributed to theatrical productions as a lighting technician. This unique blend of technical and creative skills allows me to approach problems from multiple perspectives and deliver solutions that are both functional and engaging.
